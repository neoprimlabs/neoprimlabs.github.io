What It Does

I Heart Shelling lets you log every shell, shark tooth, piece of sea glass, and treasure you find right from the beach. GPS-tag your finds, snap a photo, and build a personal collection that grows with every tide.

As your collection grows, so does your profile. Earn XP, unlock badges for finding rare species and reaching milestones, and share your best finds to a public community feed — seeing what's washing up on beaches near you in real time.

Species Encyclopedia

The built-in encyclopedia covers 100+ Gulf Coast species with descriptions, habitat notes, rarity ratings, and seasonal tips — so you always know what you're looking at, even without a signal.

Rarity ratings and seasonal data are baked into every entry, so the encyclopedia doubles as a field guide for planning when and where to go — not just identifying what you already found.

Offline First

The app works fully offline. Finds are saved locally and sync to the cloud when you're back in range, so a spotty signal at the shoreline never gets in the way. The interactive map includes offline tile support, and the beach conditions dashboard — tides, weather, water temp, and NWS alerts — is built to work in low-connectivity environments too.
