The Problem

Most project idea generators throw the same recycled suggestions at everyone — build a to-do app, build a weather app. They don't know anything about you, so they can't generate anything that's actually relevant to you.

Ship Something takes a different approach. Before generating anything, it asks you about yourself: your experience level, preferred tech stack, whether you're building solo or with a team, what domains genuinely interest you, how much time you realistically have, what's tripped you up on past projects, and how original you want to go — from proven low-risk concepts to genuinely novel ideas you'd have a hard time finding tutorials for.

The Output

That input feeds into a custom prompt built on Claude, which returns 10 project ideas tailored specifically to your answers. The ideas aren't filler — they're written to be genuinely compelling starting points.

Each idea includes:

Title + Hook
A name and one-line pitch
Description
Full writeup of what you're building
Why Build It
The case for why this idea is worth your time
Technical Systems
The interesting engineering problems involved
Suggested Stack
Recommended technologies to build with
Ratings + Scope
Difficulty, novelty, time estimate, and what "done" looks like

What You Can Do With It

  • Save ideas to a dedicated list so they don't get lost between sessions — free for all users
  • Export any idea as formatted text — copy to clipboard or share directly — free for all users
  • Refine: give the AI follow-up instructions to tighten the scope, shift the tech, or change the difficulty — unlocked permanently with first purchase

Refine isn't a separate generation — it rewrites the existing idea based on your feedback, so you're iterating toward exactly what you want rather than rolling the dice again.

Business Model

Freemium with server-side enforcement. Every account starts with 3 free generation credits — enough to experience the full core loop without paying. Additional credits are available in packs, and the first purchase permanently unlocks Refine across the account.
