Ship Something
Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Overview

NeoPrimLabs ("we," "us," or "our") built Ship Something as a freemium Android app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data.

We take your privacy seriously. If we don't need your data, we don't collect it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information via Google Sign-In (name, email, Google ID)
  • Questionnaire responses used to generate project ideas
  • Purchase history for in-app credit packs via Google Play Billing
  • Generation count and last generation ID stored locally on your device
  • Theme preference stored locally on your device

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or location data.

How We Use Your Data

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Generate personalized project ideas using the Anthropic Claude API
  • Track and enforce your generation credit balance
  • Process and verify in-app purchases

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Ship Something uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and serverless backend — supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic — AI-powered idea generation via Claude — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — account authentication — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases and purchase verification — payments.google.com/payments/apis-secure/get_legal_document?ldo=0&ldt=privacynotice

Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to provide you with the service and as described in this policy. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
